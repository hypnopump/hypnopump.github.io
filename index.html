Eric Alcaide

I'm a researcher working on LLMs, coding agents, and AI4Science.

My current research interests focus on:

  • Efficient processing of information with Recurrent Neural Networks
  • Adaptive computation & reasoning
  • Agents and LLMs for code
  • AI for Science and BioTech (3D, design, automation)

Currently, I'm a Founding Engineer at poolside. Previously, I was one of the pioneers of CoFolding early in 2021 as the first member of CHARM Tx, and worked on ML for structural biology at Vir Biotechnology. I am also an open source researcher at EleutherAI, where I led efforts on Linear Attention (author of RWKV) and AI for Science - OpenBioML community.

I hold a Physics degree and a Medicine degree (MD) from the University of Barcelona. My medical thesis, "Towards immune profiling of brain metastasis from lung cancer as a driver for precision medicine", was supervised by Ernest Nadal Alforja, MD, PhD at the ICO.
